What We Need in Morris Park

Morris Park has a lot of restaurants, pizzerias, bakeries, hair salons, nail salons, pharmacies and many more places to benefit your life. However, is there anywhere to have fun in Morris Park? Especially places to take your children other than the park. What about letting teens go out on their own? Where can they go to have fun?

Update Van Nest Lanes

Is Van Nest Lanes your favorite bowling alley to go to? Do you even know about it? You probably don't know about it because it's sort of hidden and not mentioned much. Van Nest Lanes makes less than about $500k a year. In the bowling alley you have to take your own score while you play the game and inside looks really boring. We need to update Van Nest Lanes on Bronxdale Avenue by updating the technology, seats, decorate it more and get different kinds of music to make it modern and enjoyable for the customers.

AMC Movie Theatre

Do you enjoy watching movies? Morris Park needs to build a movie theatre! Movie theatre's are usually cheap enough for teenagers to go out on their own and enjoy their night out. Not only is a movie theatre beneficial to its customers but it can also bring revenue to the area.

Dave and Buster's

A new Dave and Buster's has just been built in Pelham Manor, about 20 minutes driving wise from Morris Park. Based off of Patch.com, a few requirements to build Dave and Buster's in Pelham Manor were "No table service of alcoholic beverages anywhere in the restaurant in which an amusement device is located, no one under 16 will be permitted to operate an amusement device after 10 p.m. unless accompanied by a parent or guardian over the age of 25, the restaurant will provide for and maintain an off-duty, uniformed Pelham Manor police officer on the premises from 11 p.m. until its midnight closing every day it's open, etc." So, why can't Morris Park have a Dave and Buster's? A lot of teenagers don't have cars so why can't we build a Dave and Buster's for those in Morris Park to just walk to? Dave and Buster's would be great for family outings, grown-up fun and teenager fun!

Ice Skating Rink

Morris Park already has Loreto Playground, which has a hockey rink. I believe we should take out the hockey rink and put an ice skating rink to be enjoyable to everyone, rather than only hockey players. The ice skating rink is another attraction that can bring revenue to Morris Park.
